About the Community/Club

This club is established for gamers in Ireland specifically for Dota 2 and Clash of Clans players. It was created on the 1st of July, 2021. The goal of the community/club is to help gamers find other gamers, to have more friends and to build a strong group of like minded people to help boost the Esports industry in Ireland. The author believes that Esports is the future and will prosper in the coming years due to the inevitable technoligical advancements. In time, the author plans to expand the community to people who plays other games, to host events and tournaments. So what are you waiting for? Come one, come all and join our community and we wil prosper together through gaming.
